General Information

The breast cancer risk assessment tool is designed to estimate a woman's risk of developing invasive breast cancer.

The risk assessment is based on statistical models and should be used as a guide, not a definitive prediction.

Results Interpretation

Your risk level indicates the likelihood of developing breast cancer based on your provided information. Low risk suggests minimal likelihood, medium risk suggests moderate likelihood, and high risk suggests significant likelihood. It's important to discuss these results with a healthcare professional for a comprehensive understanding.
